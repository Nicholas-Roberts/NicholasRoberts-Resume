Bio: I was born and raised in San Diego and have lived in southern California my whole life. I graduated as an honors student from Santana High School in June of 2018 where I competed on the academic league team for four undefeated seasons. Starting the next Fall, I began attending the Univerity of California, Riverside and I am currently working on my bachelor's degree in political science. I would like to attend graduate school afterward to work on my master's degree. I intend to work in the field of psephology, a branch of political science that deals with the study of elections and voting trends which can be used to explain past elections as well as predict future ones. A major goal of mine is to one day possibly run for public office or to work on a statewide or even nationwide campaign.
Currently, while at UC Riverside, I attend the organization, College Democrats. At the time of writing this, I am the President-elect of the club, and will officially become President in June of 2020. In the club, we focus a lot on helping Democrats in the Riverside area and often have candidates running for positions like Riverside City Council, California State Assembly, or even Mayor of Riverside visit us and speak to us about the issues and their plans. In turn, we organize "get out the vote" movements and often help campaign for the candidates. As President of the student organization, I will be able to really take my first steps in a leadership position and get experience organizing events for the club and with local politicians in Riverside.
When not at Riverside for my classes, I live with my family in San Diego. My mom is a teacher at Golden Hill Elementary and Middle School and I have volunteered there, helping teachers with things like organization in their classrooms as well as moving classrooms. In more recent years, I have visited Golden Hill to talk to the kids there about government and teach them about college. It is a very rewarding experience and has taught me a lot about building communication skills, especially with kids.
